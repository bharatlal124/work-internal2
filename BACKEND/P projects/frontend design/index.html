Odoo Implementation Process

Implementing an ERP like Odoo is a marathon, not a sprint. To make this process resonate more professionally with stakeholders, we have reframed your steps into a structured, four-phase lifecycle: Strategy, Selection, Execution, and Optimization.

Strategic Alignment
Step 1
Define Goals:

Identify pain points and set KPIs.

Step 2
Plan Budget:

Secure funds for licenses and services.

Solution Selection
Step 3
Evaluate Platform:

Confirm Odoo fits your workflow.

Step 4
Pick Partner:

Choose a certified industry expert.

Technical Foundation
Step 5
Phase Roadmap:

Create a tiered project charter.

Step 6
Setup Hosting:

Choose Online, Odoo.sh, or On-premise.

Step 7
Configure System:

Tailor settings and business logic.

Deployment & Continuity
Step 8
Migrate Data:

Clean and move legacy records.

Step 9
Test (UAT):

Verify workflows in real-world scenarios.

Step 10
Train Users:

Ensure team adoption and skill transfer.

Step 11
Go-Live:

Launch with post-setup "Hypercare."

Step 12
Support:

Plan for upgrades and ongoing maintenance.
